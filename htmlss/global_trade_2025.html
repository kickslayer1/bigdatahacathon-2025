Rwanda Economic Overview 2025

Population: 13.7 million | GDP: $13.3B (2022) | GDP Growth: 8.2% (2022)

GDP per Capita: $966 | Data Source: U.S. International Trade Administration

Trade Performance Dashboard

Total Exports (2022): $2.99B (+38.5% growth from 2021)

Total Imports (2022): $4.98B (+29% growth from 2021)

US-Rwanda Bilateral Trade: $122.4M (+49.6% growth)

Tourism Revenue (2022): $445M (+171.3% recovery from 2021)

Key Export Markets

  • United Arab Emirates: 28.3% market share - Leading export destination
  • Congo DRC: 20.19% market share - Regional trade partner
  • China: 11.81% market share - Strategic partner
  • Pakistan: Growing market with significant trade potential

Principal Export Products

  • Gold: Leading export commodity
  • Coffee: Premium quality export
  • Tea: Export grade production
  • Sanitary Products: Towels & diapers
  • Steel Products: Flat-rolled steel

Economic Sectors Analysis

Services Sector: 47% of GDP with 11% annual growth (Tourism: $445M leading foreign exchange earner)

Industry Sector: 21% of GDP employing 19% of population (Mining, Construction, Manufacturing)

Agriculture Sector: Overwhelmingly rural economy with coffee, tea, and agricultural exports

Strategic Advantages

  • Low Corruption: 54th globally (Transparency International 2022) - One of Africa's 4 least corrupt nations
  • Fast Business Registration: Register in as little as 6 hours online or via RDB One Stop Centre
  • Market Access: EAC (150M consumers), COMESA (460M), AfCFTA (1.4B continental market)
  • Economic Growth: 7%+ annually for 20 years with 8.2% growth in 2022

Key Investment Opportunities

  • Infrastructure: Bugesera International Airport, logistics hubs, energy projects
  • Energy: Renewable energy, power generation, grid expansion
  • Healthcare: Medical equipment, pharmaceuticals, health technology
  • Agriculture: Value addition, processing, export-oriented farming
  • ICT: Kigali Innovation City, fintech, digital services
  • Tourism: Hotels, conference facilities, eco-tourism
